Chitrakarma - Painting/Sketching Competition

2nd April 2022
Time: 10:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m | Register Now

  • Mode: Online & Offline
  • Prizes: First - Rs. 2000 | Second - Rs. 1000
  • Last date for registrations is 31st March' 22, 11:59 pm IST.
  • For Offline:
    Acrylic Paints, Brushes, Paper, and Pencils will be provided for making the artwork.
  • People are free to make anything that they want, they can make mandalas, doodles, storytelling art, cartoons, anime, etc.
  • Entrants confirm that everyone whose submission is featured in the contest entry has given consent for the use of his/her submission in connection with this Contest and in accordance with these Terms and Conditions:
    • All artists will retain full ownership of their submissions but, by entering, you agree to allow us to use the entries in the promotion of the Infin8. Any personal information collected from the participants will be used only for the purposes of administering the contest.
    • The decision of the organising committee and the jury will be final.
  • Rules:
    • Only one art submission is allowed per person but more than one image of the same art is allowed.
    • The materials used should be listed in the submissions.
    • The judgment of the submissions will be done by the Art Club. Judgment will be based on the following criteria - skill, creativity.
    • Digital touch-ups beyond cropping and rotation are not allowed. Any additional editing of the artwork will result in disqualification.
    • Image name should be the name of the participant.
    • The submission links will be sent to registered participants via mail.
    • Results will be announced on our Instagram handle.
