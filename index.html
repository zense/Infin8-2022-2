Infin8 2022

About The Event

IIIT Bangalore is back with a BANG with its 7th iteration of INFIN8- BIGGER and BETTER than before. Hold on to your seats as we present to you the largest and most exciting fest of our institute! A plethora of events awaits you to showcase your inner artist: War of words, Esports, Digital Art, Photography, Singing and Dance competitions and a lot more! Join in and be a part of this fun ride along with us @IIIT Bangalore. See you at the starting point of a marathon of mind-boggling, rib-tickling and adrenaline pumping events!
