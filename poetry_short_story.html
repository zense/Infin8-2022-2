Spill the Ink - Poetry/Short Story Competition

Online
Register Now

Poetry is when an emotion has found it's thought and thought have found words. There is no greater agony than bearing an untold story inside you. - "Maya Angelou" Bring out the pen warriors within you to fight this mighty battle of the best poet and best story writers. We are waiting to spectate a miraculous war of thoughts . Join us in the event of imagination where we have dragons and liliputs.

  • Mode: Online
  • Prizes: Best Story - Rs.2000 | Best Poem - Rs.2000
  • Last date for registrations is 31st March' 22, 11:59 pm IST.
  • Entrants confirm that everyone whose submission is featured in the contest entry has given consent for the use of his/her submission in connection with this Contest and in accordance with these Terms and Conditions:
    • All artists will retain full ownership of their submissions but, by entering, you agree to allow us to use the entries in the promotion of the Infin8. Any personal information collected from the participants will be used only for the purposes of administering the contest.
    • The decision of the organising committee and the jury will be final.
  • Rules:
    • It must be in English.
    • It can be fictional or non-fictional.
    • It must not have been published before or submitted to another competition.
    • Story must be a maximum of 700 words or less (including the title). (All word processors have a facility for counting the number of words). Amd poem mustnot exceed 30 lines. Any story over 700 words and poem above 30 lines will not be read by the judges.
    • Give your story and poem a title.
    • Use of vulgar language will lead to disqualification.
    • Points will be awarded on the basis of innovation, depiction of the situation, creativity and use of good language.
    • The judges read all the entries; their decision is final.
    • Max 1 entry per person allowed for story writing and 2 entries for poetry writing.
    • Results will be announced on our Instagram handle.
    • The submission links will be sent to registered participants via mail.
